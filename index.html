Travel To St Moritz!

St Moritz is located in Switzerland. It is a beautiful place with lakes, Mountain ranges and so much more.

To Do's

  • Book a House to live in for some time in the Alps
  • Have Hot Chocolate in a hotel

I rate St Moritz 9/10 because everything is so good but it is a little bit to cold if you aren't wearing enough clothes!
